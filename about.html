Welcome to Swadu Cafe, located near the picturesque UPES campus in Dehradun. Our cafe is a hub for students, faculty, and locals alike, offering a warm and inviting atmosphere for you to enjoy delicious food and drinks.

At Swadu Cafe, we believe that great food should not only taste amazing but should also be made from the freshest, high-quality ingredients. That's why we work hard to source the best ingredients and prepare them with care, so that every dish is packed with flavor and nutrition.

Our menu offers a range of options to suit different tastes and preferences, from classic breakfast items and sandwiches to hearty soups and salads, as well as a variety of beverages, including freshly brewed coffee and tea, smoothies, and more.

We take pride in offering exceptional service to our customers and strive to create a welcoming and comfortable environment for you to relax and enjoy your time with us. Whether you're looking to catch up on some work, meet up with friends, or simply unwind after a long day, Swadu Cafe is the perfect destination.

So come on in and experience the taste of Swadu Cafe for yourself! We look forward to serving you.
